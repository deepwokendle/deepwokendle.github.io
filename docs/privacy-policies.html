Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google and other third-party advertising providers may use cookies and similar technologies to serve and personalize ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help the site function and to improve your user experience. On Deepwokendle cookies may be used for basic site functionality, remembering simple preferences, and allowing advertising partners to deliver ads. We do not actively use cookies ourselves for advanced tracking or profiling beyond what third-party services (such as ad networks) may set.

Third-Party Services & Data We Collect

Deepwokendle itself collects minimal information. The only user-provided data we store is a username and a hashed password (used strictly to allow you to log in). We do not require or store email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifying information for account creation. We do not believe we use user data for any other purpose beyond providing the login feature and basic site functionality.

However, third-party services used on this site (for example Google AdSense, hosting providers, or analytics tools) may collect additional information about your visit independently (such as IP address, device and browser information, or cookies). Those third parties control their own data practices — please consult their privacy policies for details.

Your Choices

If you prefer not to have cookies stored by third parties, you can disable or delete cookies through your browser settings — note that disabling cookies may affect some site features or ad personalization. To opt out of personalized ads, use the Google Ads Settings page.

If you want to request access to the account data we store (your username) or ask for removal of your account and associated hashed password, contact us (see the Contact section). We will respond to reasonable requests to delete or export data associated with your account where technically possible.

Security

We take basic measures to protect stored account credentials (for example, storing passwords hashed rather than in plain text). No security setup is perfect — if you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately and change any credentials you may have reused elsewhere.
